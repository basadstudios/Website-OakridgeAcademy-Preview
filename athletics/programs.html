How to Participate

1

Eligibility Requirements

To participate in athletics, students must:

  • Maintain a minimum GPA of 2.0
  • Complete required health forms and physical examination
  • Adhere to school attendance and behavior policies
  • Submit athletic participation forms and fees
2

Tryout Process

Most teams hold tryouts at the beginning of each season:

  • Fall sports: August
  • Winter sports: November
  • Spring sports: March
  • Tryouts typically last 3-5 days
  • Coaches evaluate skills, fitness, attitude, and team fit
3

Commitment Expectations

Team members are expected to:

  • Attend all practices (typically 5-6 days/week)
  • Participate in all games, meets, or matches
  • Maintain academic eligibility throughout the season
  • Demonstrate sportsmanship and school values
  • Contribute to team activities and fundraisers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have to try out for all teams?

Most of our varsity and JV teams require tryouts. However, some sports—particularly at the freshman level or in developing programs—may have a no-cut policy. Each program's tryout requirements are announced before the season begins.

Can students play multiple sports?

Yes, we encourage multi-sport participation. Playing multiple sports helps develop well-rounded athletes and reduces the risk of overuse injuries. Our coaches coordinate to accommodate multi-sport athletes, though participation in overlapping seasons may require prioritization.

What is the athletic fee, and what does it cover?

The athletic participation fee is $200 per sport with a family maximum of $500 per year. This fee helps cover coaching, transportation, officials, equipment, and facility maintenance. Financial assistance is available for families who qualify.

How does the school balance academics and athletics?

We maintain the perspective that our athletes are students first. We schedule practices to allow adequate time for academic work, monitor academic progress, provide support resources, and emphasize the importance of educational achievement alongside athletic development.
