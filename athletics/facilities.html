Athletic Facilities

State-of-the-art facilities that support athletic excellence and student development.

Our Athletic Facilities

At Oakridge Academy, we believe that quality facilities are essential for athletic development and team success. Our campus features modern, well-maintained facilities for all of our athletic programs, providing athletes with the ideal environment for training and competition.

Athletics Complex

Our athletics complex is designed for convenience, functionality, and athlete development. All facilities are located on campus, allowing for easy access between academic and athletic activities.

Main Athletics Complex Address:

123 Scholar Lane, Chicago, IL 60601

Public Hours:

Varies by facility. See individual facility details below.

About Our Facilities:

All facilities are maintained to the highest standards for safety, performance, and aesthetics. Our maintenance team ensures that each venue is competition-ready and represents our school with pride.

Our Facilities

Main Gymnasium

Our spacious main gymnasium features a regulation-size basketball court, volleyball court, and spectator seating for 1,200.

Key Features:

  • Maple hardwood flooring
  • Electronic scoreboard system
  • Sound system
  • Retractable bleachers
  • Climate controlled

Sports Supported:

Basketball, Volleyball, Wrestling, Physical Education

Auxiliary Gymnasium

A versatile secondary gymnasium that provides additional practice space and hosts junior varsity and freshman competitions.

Key Features:

  • Regulation-size basketball court
  • Volleyball court setup
  • Athletic flooring
  • Spectator bleachers
  • Storage for equipment

Sports Supported:

Basketball, Volleyball, Physical Education, Indoor Activities

Aquatic Center

Our state-of-the-art aquatic center includes a 25-yard, 8-lane competition pool and diving well.

Key Features:

  • 25-yard competition pool
  • 1-meter and 3-meter diving boards
  • Electronic timing system
  • Spectator seating for 300
  • Pool lift for accessibility

Sports Supported:

Swimming & Diving, Water Polo, Physical Education

Football Stadium

Our football stadium serves as the centerpiece of our outdoor athletic facilities, featuring a synthetic turf field and track.

Key Features:

  • Press box and sound system

Sports Supported:

Football, Soccer, Track & Field, Lacrosse

Facility Use Information

For Students & Teams

Our athletic facilities are primarily used for school athletic teams, physical education, and student activities. Facility use is coordinated through the Athletics Department.

  • Team Practice Schedules: Coordinated by coaches and the Athletic Director
  • Student Use: Some facilities are available during designated open hours
  • Locker Rooms: Assigned by team and season
  • Equipment Check-out: Available through team coaches or the equipment room

Community & Rental Use

Some of our athletic facilities are available for rental by community groups, youth sports organizations, and other approved groups when not in use by school programs.

  • Rental Requests: Submitted through the Athletics Office
  • Priority: School programs have priority use of all facilities
  • Insurance: Required for all outside groups
  • Supervision: School staff must be present for all facility use
