Athletics

Building character, teamwork, and excellence through competitive sports.

Athletic Philosophy

At Oakridge Academy, we believe athletics play a vital role in developing the whole student. Our program emphasizes skill development, sportsmanship, teamwork, and personal growth. We strive for competitive excellence while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and character.

With over 20 sports across three seasons, we offer opportunities for students of all abilities to participate, learn, and grow through athletics.

Core Values

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all aspects of our athletic program, encouraging our athletes to develop their skills and reach their full potential.

Teamwork

We foster collaboration, mutual respect, and the understanding that collective success requires individual sacrifice and commitment to common goals.

Character

We develop integrity, resilience, sportsmanship, and leadership through the challenges and opportunities of competitive athletics.
