Payment Options

Annual Payment

Full tuition payment due by July 1st. Families who choose this option receive a 2% discount on tuition.

Semi-Annual Payments

Two equal payments due July 1st and December 1st. This option incurs no additional fees.

Monthly Payment Plan

Ten equal monthly payments (July through April) through our tuition management service.

Financial Aid & Scholarships

Oakridge Academy is committed to making our educational programs accessible to qualified students regardless of family financial circumstances. Our need-based financial aid program supports this commitment by helping to bridge the gap between what a family can afford and the cost of tuition.

Each year, we dedicate approximately 20% of our operating budget to financial aid, serving families across a broad range of income levels. All aid is awarded based on demonstrated financial need, and all applications are kept strictly confidential.

Apply for Financial Aid

How to Apply for Financial Aid

1

Complete Financial Aid Application

Submit an application through School and Student Services (SSS), a third-party service that helps schools determine financial need. Applications open in October for the following academic year.

2

Provide Supporting Documentation

Upload your most recent tax returns, W-2 forms, and other financial documents to complete your application. All information is kept strictly confidential.

3

Application Review

Our Financial Aid Committee reviews all applications to determine awards based on demonstrated financial need and available funds.

4

Award Notification

Financial aid decisions are sent with or shortly after admission decisions. New families typically receive notifications in March.

Priority Deadline: February 1 for the upcoming academic year

Frequently Asked Questions

When is tuition due?

Tuition may be paid as a single payment due July 1, or in installments through our payment plan options. The enrollment contract outlines specific due dates for your selected payment plan.

Is financial aid available for new students?

Yes, financial aid is available for both new and returning students. All families are encouraged to apply if they believe they may need assistance with tuition costs.

How do I apply for financial aid?

Families apply for financial aid through School and Student Services (SSS), a third-party service that helps schools determine financial need. The application requires tax returns and other financial information.

When should I apply for financial aid?

For priority consideration, financial aid applications should be submitted by February 1 for the following academic year. Applications received after this date will be considered as funds remain available.

Are there additional costs beyond tuition?

Yes, there are some additional costs including mandatory fees, books, supplies, uniforms, and optional expenses like after-school programs, trips, and lunch service.

Is tuition insurance available?

Yes, we offer tuition insurance through a third-party provider which helps protect your investment in the event of a student withdrawal due to medical or other covered reasons.
