Welcome to Our Campus

Visiting our campus is the best way to experience the Oakridge Academy community. You'll have the opportunity to tour our facilities, meet our faculty and students, and learn about our academic programs and extracurricular activities.

Campus Tours

Take a guided tour of our campus to see our facilities, meet our staff, and learn about our programs.

Available Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 2:00 PM, by appointment

Open House Events

Attend an open house to learn about our academic programs, extracurricular activities, and admissions process.

Held monthly throughout the fall and winter

Shadow Days

Prospective students can spend a day with a current student, attending classes and experiencing a typical day.

Available for students in grades 6-12, by appointment

Virtual Tours

Explore our campus and learn about our programs from the comfort of your home through a virtual tour.

Available on-demand or as scheduled virtual events

Campus Location

Interactive Map

Find Us

Oakridge Academy is conveniently located at 123 Education Blvd, Springfield, IL 60602.

Directions

From Interstate 95: Take exit 42B. Turn right onto Education Blvd. The school entrance will be on your left after 0.5 miles.

Visitor Hours

Our campus is open to visitors Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Visitors must check in at the main office.
