About Oakridge

Inspiring Future Leaders

Oakridge Academy was founded in 2010 with a vision to redefine education for the modern world. What began as a small school with just 50 students has grown into a thriving educational community serving hundreds of students from kindergarten through high school.

Our founder, Dr. James Anderson, a renowned educator and educational psychologist, established Oakridge Academy based on the principle that education should nurture not just academic achievement, but also critical thinking, creativity, character, and social responsibility.

Academic Excellence

Our rigorous curriculum and dedicated faculty ensure students achieve their highest academic potential.

Character Development

We nurture not just academic achievement, but also integrity, responsibility, and leadership skills.

Technology Integration

State-of-the-art technology is integrated throughout our curriculum to prepare students for the digital future.

Innovative Programs

Our STEM, arts, and athletics programs provide students with well-rounded educational experiences.
