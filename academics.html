Academics

A curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares students for success in college and beyond.

Our Educational Approach

Academic Excellence

At Oakridge Academy, we provide a challenging and supportive educational experience that develops intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a passion for lifelong learning.

Educational Philosophy

Our educational approach is built on the belief that students learn best when they are actively engaged, appropriately challenged, and supported by caring educators who recognize their individual strengths and needs.

We emphasize depth of understanding over breadth of content, encouraging students to think critically, solve complex problems, and make connections across disciplines. Our curriculum balances traditional academic rigor with innovative approaches that prepare students for a rapidly changing world.

Small class sizes allow our teachers to know each student as an individual and tailor instruction to support their growth. We nurture not only academic achievement but also character development, creativity, and social responsibility.

Academic Programs

STEM

Our rigorous STEM program emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation through advanced courses in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

  • •Advanced Placement and Honors courses in Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Environmental Science
  • •Computer Science pathway including Programming, Data Structures, and AI/Machine Learning
  • •Engineering Design and Robotics programs with competitive teams
  • •Mathematics curriculum through Calculus BC and Statistics
Learn more about STEM

Humanities

Our humanities program develops strong communication skills, cultural understanding, and ethical reasoning through literature, history, philosophy, and the arts.

  • •Literature studies spanning diverse authors, periods, and cultures
  • •History coursework examining global perspectives and critical analysis
  • •Philosophy and Ethics seminars fostering deeper understanding of complex issues
  • •Communication skills development through debate, public speaking, and writing
Learn more about Humanities

Arts

Our comprehensive arts program nurtures creativity, self-expression, and technical skill through visual arts, music, theater, and digital media.

  • •Studio Art, Sculpture, and Digital Design with portfolio development
  • •Music education including Orchestra, Jazz Ensemble, and Music Theory
  • •Theater program with acting, directing, and technical production
  • •Annual art exhibitions, concerts, and performances
Learn more about Arts

Global Education

Our global education program prepares students for an interconnected world through language acquisition, cultural studies, and international experiences.

  • •Language offerings in Spanish, French, Mandarin, and Latin
  • •Global Affairs curriculum with focus on contemporary international issues
  • •Cultural exchange programs with partner schools worldwide
  • •Model United Nations and international service learning opportunities
Learn more about Global Education
