About this Event

Representatives from various colleges and universities will be available to discuss admissions, financial aid, and academic programs with our high school students and parents.

We invite all members of our school community to join us for this special event. Whether you are a student, parent, faculty member, or friend of Oakridge Academy, this is an opportunity to engage with our vibrant school culture and connect with others.

What to Expect

  • Engaging activities for all attendees
  • Opportunities to meet with faculty and staff
  • Refreshments will be provided
  • Ample parking available on campus
