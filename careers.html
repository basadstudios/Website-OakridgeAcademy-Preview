Current Openings

Executive Chef

KitchenMain RestaurantFull-time

We are looking for an experienced Executive Chef to lead our kitchen team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for Italian cuisine and a proven track record of managing a successful kitchen operation.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and implement new menu items
  • Manage kitchen staff and operations
  • Ensure food quality and consistency
  • Control food costs and inventory
  • Maintain health and safety standards

Requirements:

  • Minimum 5 years of experience as a Chef in a high-end restaurant
  • Culinary degree or equivalent experience
  • Strong leadership and management skills
  • Knowledge of Italian cuisine
  • Food safety certification

Restaurant Manager

OperationsMain RestaurantFull-time

We are seeking a Restaurant Manager to oversee the daily operations of our restaurant. The ideal candidate will have excellent leadership skills and a passion for delivering exceptional customer service.

Responsibilities:

  • Oversee daily restaurant operations
  • Manage front-of-house staff
  • Ensure high standards of food and service quality
  • Handle customer inquiries and resolve issues
  • Manage budgets and financial performance

Requirements:

  • Minimum 3 years of experience in restaurant management
  • Strong leadership and organizational skills
  • Excellent customer service skills
  • Knowledge of restaurant operations and procedures
  • Experience with scheduling and inventory management

Server

Front of HouseMain RestaurantPart-time

We are looking for friendly and professional Servers to join our team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for food and providing excellent customer service.

Responsibilities:

  • Take customer orders and deliver food and beverages
  • Make recommendations and answer questions about menu items
  • Ensure customer satisfaction and a positive dining experience
  • Process payments and maintain accurate cash handling
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization of dining areas

Requirements:

  • Previous experience as a server in a restaurant setting
  • Knowledge of food and beverage preparation and service
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Availability to work evenings, weekends, and holidays
