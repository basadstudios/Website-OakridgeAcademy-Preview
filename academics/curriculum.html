Curriculum

Our comprehensive curriculum is designed to challenge, engage, and inspire students at every grade level, preparing them for success in college and beyond.

Our Educational Approach

What We Believe

We believe that learning should be challenging, engaging, and relevant to students' lives. Our curriculum balances academic rigor with creativity, critical thinking, and real-world application, fostering a lifelong love of learning.

We recognize that each student learns differently, and we provide a variety of instructional approaches to accommodate diverse learning styles and needs, ensuring all students can reach their full potential.

How We Teach

  • •Small class sizes for personalized attention
  • •Interdisciplinary learning to connect subjects
  • •Project-based learning for practical application
  • •Technology integration for digital literacy
  • •Collaborative learning to develop teamwork
  • •Regular assessment and feedback for growth

Our Lower School program lays a strong foundation in core subjects while fostering curiosity, creativity, and a love of learning. Students develop fundamental skills in reading, writing, mathematics, and scientific inquiry through engaging, hands-on activities and personalized instruction.

College Preparation

Our college preparatory program equips students with the knowledge, skills, and habits necessary for success in selective colleges and universities. Through rigorous academics, standardized test preparation, and personalized college counseling, we support each student throughout the application process.
