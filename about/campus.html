Where Learning Happens

Our Campus

The Oakridge Academy campus provides an inspiring environment designed to support our educational mission and enhance student learning experiences.

Academic Buildings

Our academic buildings feature modern classrooms equipped with the latest technology to support innovative teaching methods.

Science & Technology Labs

State-of-the-art laboratories provide hands-on learning experiences in science, robotics, and computer science.

Library & Learning Commons

Our library combines traditional resources with digital tools and collaborative spaces to support research and group projects.

Athletic Facilities

Comprehensive athletic facilities include gymnasiums, fields, courts, and training areas for our diverse sports programs.

Outdoor Spaces

Beautiful grounds provide outdoor learning environments, recreational areas, and spaces for community gatherings.

Campus Location

Conveniently located in Springfield, our campus is easily accessible while providing a secure and focused learning environment.

Campus Map & Directions

Our campus is located at 123 Education Blvd, Springfield, IL 60602. Visitor parking is available in the main lot adjacent to the Administration Building.

Interactive Campus Map

Directions

  • •From Interstate 95: Take exit 42B. Turn right onto Education Blvd. The school entrance will be on your left after 0.5 miles.
  • •From Downtown: Follow Main Street north for 2 miles. Turn left onto Scholar Avenue, then right onto Education Blvd.
  • •Public Transportation: Bus routes 12 and 17 stop directly in front of the school entrance.

Campus Hours

  • Monday - Friday:7:30 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Saturday:Closed (Open for special events)
  • Sunday:Closed

Administrative Office Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:30 PM
