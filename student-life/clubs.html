Starting a New Club

Do you have an interest or passion that isn't represented in our current club offerings? Students are encouraged to propose new clubs that align with our school's mission and values.

How to Start a Club:

  1. Find a faculty advisor willing to sponsor and supervise the club
  2. Recruit at least 5 interested students
  3. Complete the Club Proposal Form outlining the club's purpose, goals, and activities
  4. Submit the proposal to the Student Activities Office
  5. Present your proposal to the Student Activities Committee

Frequently Asked Questions

How many clubs can I join?

Students are encouraged to explore their interests, but we recommend limiting active participation to 2-3 clubs to ensure you can fully commit to each one. Quality of involvement is more important than quantity.

When do clubs meet?

Most clubs meet after school from 3:30-4:30 PM or 3:30-5:00 PM once or twice per week. Some clubs may also meet during lunch or before school, depending on the advisor's and members' schedules.

How do I join a club?

Most clubs have an open enrollment period during the first two weeks of each semester. Simply attend a meeting during this period to sign up. Some competitive or performance-based clubs may require auditions or applications.

Do clubs cost money to join?

Most clubs are free to join and are funded through the school's activities budget. Some clubs may have minimal fees for special materials, travel, or competition registration. Financial aid is available for these costs.

Questions About Clubs & Activities?

Contact the Student Activities Office for more information about clubs, activities, and how to get involved.
